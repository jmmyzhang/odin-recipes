Lasagna Recipe

Description

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. BONUS: it's make-ahead and freezer-friendly!

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 0.75 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 tsps Italian seasoning
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 0.75 lb ground beef
  • 0.75 lb ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 0.5 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine cheese filling ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat and add diced onions, soften for 10 minutes.
  3. Add ground beef and sausage and increase heat to medium-high, breaking up meat finely.
  4. Once cooked through, add garlic for another minute, then drain excess grease.
  5. Add chicken broth, marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer
  7. Preheat oven to 375 C.
  8. Boil large pot of salted water and cook noodles to al dente according to package instructions.
  9. Drain and rinse noodles with cold water until completely cool.
  10. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch casserole dish, then adding 4 lasagna noodles.
  11. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over noodles, followed by 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce.
  12. Top with 4 noodles, 1/3 ricotta mixture, and 1 1/2 cups meat sauce.
  13. Finish with 4 more noodles along with the remainder of the ricotta mixture and the meat sauce.
  14. Top with 2 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese.
  15. Cover casserole dish with foil.
  16. Bake for 35 minutes, then remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes.
  17. Garnish with fresh parsley, letting sit for 15 minutes prior to serving.
