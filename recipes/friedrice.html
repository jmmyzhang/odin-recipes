Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Learn how to make fried rice with this classic recipe. It only takes 15 minutes to make, it’s easy to customize with your favorite add-ins, and it's SO flavorful and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsps butter
  • 2 large eggs, whisked
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 1 small white onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt & black pepper
  • 4 cups leftover rice
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tbsps soy sauce
  • 2 tsps oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat 1/2 tbsp butter in pan over medium-high heat, scramble egg until cooked and set aside.
  2. Add another 1 tbsp butter into pan, then add carrots, onion, peas and garlic, season with salt and pepper and sauté for 5 minutes.
  3. Set heat to high, then add remaining 1 1/2 tbsps butter, followed immediately by rice, green onions, soy sauce and oyster sauce, then stir until combined.
  4. Continue sautéing for 3 more minutes to fry the rice, stirring occasionally. Add in egs and stir to combine.
  5. Remove from heat, and stir in sesame oil until combined. Taste and season as needed.
  6. Serve immediately, or refrigerate in a sealed container for up to 3 days.
